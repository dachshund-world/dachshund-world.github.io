Contact Dachshund World

Dachshund World covers a breed with specific health sensitivities, and getting the information right matters. If anything on this site is incorrect — particularly on IVDD, harness recommendations, or back health — the Dachshund World Content Editors want to hear about it promptly.

We also hear from readers who are in the middle of an IVDD situation and looking for clarity. While we cannot provide individual medical advice, we can point you to the most relevant content on the site and be direct about where a vet's input is needed rather than a guide.

How to Reach Us

Contact the editorial panel at: hello@dachshund-world.github.io

For corrections, tell us the article and the specific claim. For general dachshund questions, just write — and if it's an urgent situation involving your dog's movement or pain, please contact your vet first rather than waiting for an email reply.

Dachshund World does not accept advertising pitches or commercial collaboration requests through this channel.
