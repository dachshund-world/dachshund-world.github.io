How Dachshund World Is Funded

Dachshund World earns revenue through Amazon affiliate links. We want to be straightforward about that, including what it means for the product recommendations you read here.

Affiliate Links Explained

Certain product links within our guides — particularly those related to harnesses, ramps, and other dachshund health and safety products — are Amazon affiliate links. When you follow one of these links and complete a purchase, Dachshund World receives a commission from Amazon. The amount you pay does not change; affiliate commissions are paid from Amazon's own revenue share, not from your purchase price.

Harnesses, Ramps, and Safety Products

Because dachshunds have specific spinal health needs, a significant portion of our product mentions involve items that affect back health — step-in harnesses, ramps and steps, orthopedic beds, and similar products. Our editorial panel selects these products based on suitability for a long-bodied, chondrodystrophic breed. Commission rates do not factor into these recommendations. A step-in harness that is safer for a dachshund's spine is recommended over one with a higher commission if the evidence supports that preference.

Editorial Independence

The Dachshund World Content Editors make all product and content decisions independently of any commercial consideration. No manufacturer pays to be featured on this site. No brand has a commercial relationship with the editorial panel that influences what we write. Affiliate income supports the site's operation — it does not direct its content.

Regulatory Compliance

This page satisfies disclosure requirements under Federal Trade Commission guidelines (16 CFR Part 255). Dachshund World is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases made through our links.

No Paid Editorial Content

There are no sponsored posts on Dachshund World. There are no paid reviews or advertorials. No one can pay to improve their product's treatment in our content. The editorial team's focus on IVDD safety and dachshund welfare is non-negotiable and not subject to commercial arrangement.
