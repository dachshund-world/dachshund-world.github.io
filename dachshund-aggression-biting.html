Why Do Dachshunds Bite? How to Stop Aggression

25% of dachshunds show aggression. This guide covers root causes and training solutions.

Understanding Dachshund Aggression

Dachshund aggression is one of the most common behavior problems in the breed.

Root Causes

  • Fear and Anxiety (40%)
  • Resource Guarding (30%)
  • Territorial Aggression (20%)

Treatment Solutions

Desensitization and counter-conditioning are most effective.
